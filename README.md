Krea is the all-in-one platform for African creators who sell their knowledge. Authors, coaches, and trainers can create, sell, and protect their ebooks. No raw downloadable PDF. Everything is read inside a secure reader with personal watermark, device limits, and anti-piracy protection.

## Features

### For creators
- **Built-in ebook editor**: Write chapter by chapter in Markdown, generate covers, publish in one click
- **Customizable website**: Your own site at your name (`krea.com/your-name`) with 6 themes, 4 fonts, 3 layouts, custom pages, and controllable footer
- **Full dashboard**: Sales stats, revenue, charts, buyer geolocation, withdrawal management
- **Mobile Money**: Get paid via MTN, Orange, Wave, and bank card. Withdraw within 24h in FCFA from 10,000 F
- **Sales tools**: Promo coupons, affiliate links, multi-ebook bundles, optimized sales pages
- **Real-time wallet**: Your sales credited instantly, withdraw whenever you want

### For readers
- **Secure library**: All your purchased ebooks in one place
- **Protected reader**: Personal watermark on every page, no PDF download
- **Bookmarks & highlights**: Resume reading where you left off
- **Reading stats**: Track your progress, monthly goals, reading time

### Anti-piracy protection
- **Social watermark**: Buyer's name, email, and phone are embedded on every page
- **Flattening**: Pages are converted to images, watermark cannot be erased
- **Steganography**: An invisible watermark is hidden in metadata
- **Device limit**: Access limited to 3 devices, account sharing is detected
