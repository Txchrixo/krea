     4→**Phase:** Initial build (design system + schema + landing + app shell)

     6→## Context
     7→Krea is a marketplace platform for African/francophone creators to create, sell, and protect ebooks.
     8→Buyers read inside a secured reader (watermark, device limits, no direct PDF). Monetization via
     9→commissions + creator subscriptions + premium services + affiliation. Mobile Money payment flow.
